About Us

We are a team of two dedicated students from the National University of Singapore (NUS) with a shared passion for website development and financial empowerment. As residents of Singapore, we are acutely aware of the challenges posed by the rising cost of living and an aging population.

Driven by our desire to make a meaningful impact, we have created this platform to help the aging population secure a financially stable and comfortable retirement. Our mission is to provide accessible, user-friendly tools and resources that empower seniors to make informed financial decisions, ensuring their golden years are truly golden.
